CryptoQuest: The Shards of Genesis Book NFT

Discover the exclusive CryptoQuest Book NFT series, each representing a unique chapter, character, location, element, and rarity within the CryptoQuest universe. Own a piece of the story and earn rewards!

Our NFT smart contract ensures true ownership, allowing players to buy, sell, and trade their books securely on the blockchain. With roles for pausing, minting, upgrading, and managing royalties, our system is robust and adaptable.

  • Pauser Role: Pause and unpause the contract to control minting and transfers.
  • Minter Role: Mint new NFTs with unique metadata and attributes.
  • Royalty Manager Role: Set and manage royalty fees for secondary sales.
  • Book Manager Role: Add new books to the collection with detailed descriptions and media.
  • Publisher Role: Publish books, making them available for players.
